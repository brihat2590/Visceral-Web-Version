# Visceral Design System

## Product Context
Visceral is a trading education and paper-trading product. The landing page should feel like a premium SaaS product, not a hype-heavy crypto page. The user goal is to understand the product quickly and convert into login/signup.

## Brand Direction for Landing Page
- Primary aesthetic: monochrome black and white
- Secondary palette: neutral grays only
- Avoid saturated accent colors, especially red
- Overall tone: confident, editorial, premium, disciplined
- Visual language: SaaS marketing, restrained motion, strong hierarchy, crisp data-inspired panels

## Typography
- App shell currently uses Geist via `src/app/layout.tsx`
- Some landing sections use bold display styling and mono labels
- Preferred landing-page hierarchy:
  - Large bold headline
  - Smaller supporting paragraph
  - Small uppercase label text for metadata and section headers
  - Monospace or condensed-feeling labels are acceptable if subtle

## Layout Patterns
- Full-width landing page with centered max-width content
- Hero first, then social proof / value explanation / product sections
- Use grid-based feature cards and clear CTA clusters
- Prefer generous spacing, strong alignment, and visible section separation
- Use subtle borders, thin dividers, soft shadows, and monochrome glass only if needed

## Components
- Navbar with logo, simple nav links, and login CTA
- Hero with headline, subheadline, primary CTA, secondary CTA, and product mockup
- Process / how-it-works section with step tabs or cards
- Feature/value section with concise proof points
- Footer with legal links and brand mark

## Motion
- Minimal motion only
- Use fade/slide transitions for section reveal and tab swaps
- Avoid flashy glow, neon, or heavy parallax
- Motion should reinforce clarity, not distract

## Surface Rules for This Request
- Remove or visually suppress video-backed hero treatment
- Replace red accents with grayscale contrast
- Make buttons, cards, and sections feel like a polished SaaS landing page
- Keep branding intact through the logo and name, but modernize the presentation
